Whatâ€™s all this About

PAPERS is a suited environment to create research work with ease.

You can write white papers, research documents and other journals with intuitively built editor with an AI assistant that helps you with citations and reference searches. You can co-create your work with your fellow researchers in a real-time and version controlled manner. Invite experts to add comments and connect with peers in your field. Own and protect your work with Blockchain based content identification and Digital signage.

While writing a paper, letâ€™s focus only on the "paper" and not the "paperwork"!

Ways to sponsor your Paper

Marketplace
Buy & Sell Your research works Monetise your papers: Research Paper, White Paper, e-Books, Educational Papers, Guide Books, Journals, etc. We have a marketplace built for you to price and lock for paid access.
Grants
Publish your work abstract to look for grants The project focuses on developing network of educational institutions, government entities and corporates for sponsorships of posted projects. Browse from a list of open grants or just post your paper for open community voting.
Open Edits
Invite others to fork and build up on your paper. The requester submits funds to fork your work and as long as they loan your work you earn a yield. People using or building-on your research for a short time can loan your work and submit contributions to co-create and co-earn by adding value.
Survey Bounties
Take part in other research work with survey bounties. Browse through other project requirements according to your expertise and help researchers with data to earn credits, contribution score and build your reputation.

Maketplace
Buy & Sell Your research works

Research is an Intellectual Property; "IP", that can be monetized and listed for paid downloads on the Papers marketplace.
